Notes

The patterns of the 600-cell have been taken from M.Waegell, P.K. Aravind, N.D.Megill and M.Pavicic, Found Phys 41, 883–904 (2011).

P.Lisonek, R.Raussendorf, and V.Singh, arXiv:1401.3035 showed that the 600-cell has 233 “parity proofs” in it (the technical term for the patterns shown here). The horizontal row of boxes at the bottom of the table allow all these patterns to be seen.

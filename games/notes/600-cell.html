Notes

The table for the 600-cell and the patterns in it have been taken from M.Waegell, P.K. Aravind, N.D.Megill and M.Pavicic, “Parity Proofs of the Bell-Kochen-Specker Theorem Based on the 600-cell.” Found Phys 41, 883–904 (2011).

P.Lisonek, R.Raussendorf, and V.Singh, arXiv:1401.3035 have shown that the 600-cell has 233 “parity proofs” in it (this is the technical term for the patterns shown here). However, many of the patterns have smaller patterns embedded in them and so are not "irreducible" or elementarry. The exact number of elementary patterns is not known, but runs into the millions, at the very least.
